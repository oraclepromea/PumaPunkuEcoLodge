About Puma Punku Eco Lodge

Nestled on the mystical Isla del Sol, our eco-lodge offers an authentic Bolivian experience

Your Gateway to Ancient Bolivia

Located on the sacred Isla del Sol (Island of the Sun) in Lake Titicaca, Puma Punku Eco Lodge is your perfect base for exploring one of South America's most mystical destinations. Our lodge combines traditional Bolivian hospitality with modern comfort, offering guests an unforgettable experience at 3,812 meters above sea level.
